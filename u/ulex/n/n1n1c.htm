Iº was just passing the time of day with Old Troy of the DMP at the corner of Arbour hill there and be damned but a bloody sweep came along and he near put his gear into my eye. I turned around to let him have the weight of my tongue and who should I see coming along out of Stony Batter but Joe Hynes.
