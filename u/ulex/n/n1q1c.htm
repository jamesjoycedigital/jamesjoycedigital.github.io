The fashionable world attended en masse this morning at The wedding of — —, Grand High Chief ranger, Irish National Foresters with Miss Fir Conifer of Pine Valley. The bride looked very charming a creationº green mercerised silk, moulded on an underslip of twilight grey, sashed with an emerald yoke and finished with a triple flounce of darker hued fringe, the scheme being relieved with bretelles and hip insertions of acorn bronze. The bridesmaids, Miss Larch Conifer and Miss Spruce Conifer, sisters of the bride, wore pretty costumes in the same tone, a dainty motif of rose being worked into the pleats in a pinstripe and repeated capriciously in the jadegreen toques in the form of heron feathers of pale coral.
