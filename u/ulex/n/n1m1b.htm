Theº figure seated on a large threelegged stool was that of a broadshouldered, deepchested, stronglimbed, blueeyed, redhaired, shaggybearded, widemouthed, largenosed, longheaded, deepvoiced, barekneed, brawnyhanded, hairylegged, ruddyfaced, sinewyarmed hero. From shoulder to shoulder he measured several ells and his eggshaped knees covered wi, as was the rest of his body wherever visible, with a strong growth of tawny prickly hair similar to the mountain gorse,fucta montanis. The widewinged nostrils, from which bristles of the same tawny hue projected, were of such capacity that in within their cavernous obscurity the fieldlark might easily have lodged her nest. The eyes in which a tear and a smile strove ever for the mastery were of the dimension of a goodsized cauliflower. A powerful current of warm steaming breath issued at regular intervals from the profound cavity of his mouth while in rhythmic regularity the loud strong hale reverberations of his heart resounded deeply causing the ground and the walls of the building to vibrate and tremble not unpleasantly.

He wore a long sleeveless garment of saffron wool, reaching to the knees in a loose kilt, and this was bound about his waist with a girdle of plaited rushes. Beneath this he wore trews of deerskin, cunningly sewn with gut. Across his shoulders was flung a

A javelin spear of pointed granite rested near him while at his feet reposed a savage animal of the dog tribe whose stertorous snoring announced that he was sunk in uneasy slumber, a fact confirmed by repressed growls and convulsive movements which his master silenced from time to time by quieting blows of a formidable cudgel fashioned out of paleolithic stone.
