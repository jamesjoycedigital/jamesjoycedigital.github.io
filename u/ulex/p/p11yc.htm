Agendathº is a waste land, a home of screechowls and the sandblind upupa: Netaim, the golden, is no more. What voice rises from Netaim, from Agendath, the derelict. Lo, they come, muttering thunder of rebellion. The ghosts of beasts are trooping. And their moan shakes the earth. Elk & yak, mammoth & mastodon, the bulls of Bashan and of Babylon, troop to this sunken sea Huuh! Hark! Huuh! There is one behind that goads them to their doom flourishing a lash of scorpions & the lightnings that dart about his head are lancinating scorpions. Ominous and host of the zodiac, they moan, passing septemtrional to the septemtrion in the clouds, horned & capricorned, tusked and stony antlered multitude, murderers of the sun.
