Preparatory to anything else Mr Bloom brushed off the greater bulk of the shavings and handed Stephen the hat and ashplant and bucked him up generally in good Samaritan fashion, which he very badly needed. His (Stephen's) mind was not exactly what you would call wandering but a bit unsteady and on his expressed desire for something to drink Mr Bloom, in view of the hour it was and there being no pump of Vartry water available for their ablutions, let alone drinking purposes, hit upon an expedient by suggesting the propriety of the cabman's shelter, as it was called, near Butt Bridge. But how to get there was the rub. For the nonce he was rather nonplussed but inasmuch as the duty devolved upon him he pondered ways and means during which Stephen repeatedly yawned.
