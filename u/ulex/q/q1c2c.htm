My age. A natural phenomenon. The flesh. The image of Lotty Clarke whom I had seen at her night toilet. I respect real virtue. Whenever I set eyes on artless blushes I always looked away. A girl was standing before the monkey cages in the zoo watching a lustful chimpanzee. Her hands were crossed at her groin. I stared at them with meaning. She blushed deeply, artlessly. I looked away.
