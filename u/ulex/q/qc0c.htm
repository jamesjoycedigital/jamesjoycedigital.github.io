Heaven speed you, rural Shaun on your pilgrimage in the antipodes to the past, stout fellow, heart & soul, you are of Shamrogueshire. May your bawny hair grow fairer & rarer, our own whiteheaded boy! Just by nature & natural by design you will be longed for & looked after while you are away. as the beam of light we follow receding. you, who so often delivered us a drop of great joy dear pennyatimer into our never too late to post box. Life, it is true, will be a dream without you. a slip of the time between a date and a date. from tonight to yesterday. Alas how they murmur against him in all beds and all bunks two abed & three abed, in lodginghouses & blank But you did your 10 furlong mile in swell slapstick style. And already the sombrer portion of the gloom is gone. Brave, brave footsore Sir Shaun! The silent cock shall crow at last and the west will make the east awake. Walk while you have the night for the noon cometh wherein every post shall sleep.
