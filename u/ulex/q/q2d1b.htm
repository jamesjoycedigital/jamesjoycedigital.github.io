The Crowd

Throwaway! Even Money the Nightmare.

A dark fleet horse, unridden, flees like a phantom, his mane foaming, his eyeballs stars. The field follows. Skeleton horses ridden by dwarfs in armour., The Crowd skeletons leaping, leaping in their, in their saddles. They pass rattling. Last on a brokenwinded nag, caparisoned with the Union Jack, unread Garrett Deasy, honey cap, red jacket, orange sleeves, Garrett Deasy in the saddle, a hockeystick in his hand

The Crowd

Soft day, your honour

Garrett Deasy

(fiercely) as he passes inside the winning post) Per vias rectas!

(A douche of mutton broth falls violently over him, speckling him with sippets of carrots, onions, potatoes, turnips. The horse shies & unseats him)

The Crowd

(with a outburst) Soft day, yr honour.
