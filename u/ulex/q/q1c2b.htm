Bloom

(abjectly) Yes. Enemas I have administered. One third of a pint of infusion of quassia to which a tablespoonful of rock salt had been added. Yes. Also, I admit that I have paid homage too there where the back changes its name.

The Nymph

(withdrawing her hands from her face) But the face is the mirror of woman's soul, her tenderness, her love, her beauty. Mortals, gaze on me.

Bloom

(starts violently to his feet) You are no immortal. Be gone.
