Professor Maghinni inserts leg on toepoint of which spins his silk hat. With a deft jerk he sends it to his crown and, sidewise hatted, & parts the curtains and presents himself. He wears a slate caftan frockcoat with vermillion silk lapels, gorget of cream tulle a green lowcut waistcoat, stock collar with white kerchief tie, tight lavender trousers, patent pumps, canary gloves. In his buttonhole he has an immense blue dahlia, in his left eye a flashing eyeglass. He places his hand lightly on his chest and bows. Then minuets in advance three paces.

Maghinni

(murmurs) The poetry of motion is the art of calisthenics. No connection with Madame Legget Byrne or Levinstone's dancing academy. Fancy dress balls arranged. Katty Lanner deportment school So. The evening hours advance in step out, dispersed, lagging: languideyed, their cheeks delicate with cipria powder & false faint bloom They are in grey gauze with darker bat sleeves.

Maghinni

Avant huit! Traversé.

The night hours steal, one by one, to the last place. They are in black, masked, with daggered hair. Each has a bracelet of dull bells. Fatigued, they curchycurchy under veils.
