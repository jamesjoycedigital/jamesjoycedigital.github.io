The ULYSSES Volume

Navigation

Primary

1. Select an Episode from the dropdown menu above. [Note the menu is scrollable]. This will take you to the top level (published) draft of the chosen episode, our default starting point for exploration of the text.

2. The side menu then presents links to all available earlier levels of the episode. These include: editions, proofs, typescripts, faircopy, and protodrafts. Also available here is the 'isotext', our synoptic edition.

3. 'Notons' (referencing Joyce's use of notebook units) and 'Footnotes' (providing details of textual variants) are hyperlinked throughout. Noton links are indicated in blue and footnotes by the º symbol. Notons and footnotes are 'on' by default. You may turn them 'off' if you prefer a clear-reading view.

Note: dropdown menus and side menus scroll and collapse in order to accommodate variously sized devices.

Additional Options

* Selecting an Episode from the 'Draft Analysis' dropdown menu takes you to a detailed account of the compositional history of the episode.

* Clicking on 'Notebooks' leads you to transcriptions of the notebooks and notesheets, to a chronology of their composition, and to a list of sources.

* The 'Appendix' contains four chronological lists related to Ulysses: publications, letters, addresses, and compositional history.
