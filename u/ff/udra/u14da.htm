Oxen Sections
Using Joyce's own numeration in the manuscripts, the episode can be divided into sections: a prologue, the nine months of gestation, and an epilogue.
  • (–)     U84 14.1–U84 14.1-70
  • (I)      U84 14.71–U84 14.122
  • (II)     U84 14.123–U84 14.276
  • (III)    U84 14.277–U84 14.428
  • (IV)    U84 14.429–U84 14.473
  • (V)     U84 14.474–U84 14.528
  • (VI)    U84 14.529–U84 14.650
  • (VII)   U84 14.651–U84 14.844
  • (VIII)  U84 14.845–U84 14.1109
  • (IX)    U84 14.1110–U84 14.1439
  • (–)     U84 14.1440–U84 14.1591

Early Drafts

Buffalo MS V.A.11:
School exercise book (Trieste) with purple paper covers. [10] leaves of unlined paper, 23.8 x 19.5 cm. Text in Joyce's hand in black ink on rectos, with pencil and ink revisions in margins and on facing versos. The rectos are numbered 1-10 by Joyce. “OXEN OF THE SUN” handwritten on label on front cover
Buffalo MS V.A.12:
School exercise book with tan paper covers. [16] leaves of ruled paper, 22.7 x 16.2 cm. Text in Joyce's hand in black ink on rectos, with pencil and ink revisions in margins and on facing versos. The rectos are on leaves 1-13 are numbered 11-23 by Joyce.
NLI Add MS 36,639/11A (NLI.11A):
School exercise book (Trieste) with purple paper covers. [10] leaves of unlined paper, 23.8 x 19.5 cm. Text in Joyce's hand in black ink on rectos, with pencil and ink revisions in margins and on facing versos. The first 6 rectos are numbered 1-6 by Joyce, 7 and 8 are unnumbered, 9 and 10 blank.
NLI Add MS 36,639/11B (NLI.11B):
School exercise book with marbled purple-grey covers. [20] leaves of ruled paper, 20.4 x 15.2 cm. Text (discontinuous) in Joyce's hand in black ink on rectos, with pencil and ink revisions in margins and on facing versos. The leaves are unnumbered, and several pages are blank.
