Early Drafts

u10.0 (eo-text)
First draft: missing.
  • MS: missing
u10.1 (proto-text)
Probably a fair copy (missing).
  • MS: missing

Fair Copy

u10.2 (Rosenbach)
Final fair copy (ink); Zurich, end January 1919
  • MS:Rosenbach Museum, fols. numbered 1-31, 1-17 (32-48)
  • Reproduced: A Facsimile of the Manuscript (Faber, 1975): vol. I
  • Notebooks used: UN1 (NLI.3), UN2 (VI.D.7), UN3 (VIII.A.7)

Typescript

Only one typescript (see Buffalo V.A.8) for this episode was made, although the evidence suggests that the typing was authorially supervised to some extent.
u10.3   (Gabler—B)
First typescript: exemplar (missing) prepared and revised for publication in The Little Review (June 1919, pt 1, and July 1919, pt 2); revisions made early 1919.
  • MS: missing
u10.3''   (Gabler—B)
First typescript: third exemplar (incomplete); only lightly corrected
  • MS:V.B.8.b missing, 6-10, missing
  • Reproduced: JJA 13: 2-6
u10.3'   (Gabler—B)
First typescript: exemplar prepared and revised for publication (first part only, corresponding to U84 10.1-396) in The Egoist (December 1919); this MS was probably returned to Joyce to be used again in 1921 as part of the copy for Darantiere (see 10.4 below); the revisions at this stage were made early 1918.
  • MS: missing
u10.4   (Gabler—C)
First typescript: stamped “12Dec Recd” (possibly by the Egoist printer); exemplar made in early 1919 but prepared for the printer of Ulysses in 1921.
  • MS Buffalo V.B.8.a: 1-2, private 3, V.B.8.a4-24, missing 25
  • Reproduced: JJA 13: 8-9, 10-30, not reproduced
  • Notebooks used: UN1 (NLI.3), UN4 (NLI.5A)
