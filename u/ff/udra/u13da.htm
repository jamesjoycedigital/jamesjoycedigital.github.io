Early Drafts

u13.0 (eo-text)
First draft: missing.
  • MS: missing
Buffalo MS V.A.10:
School exercise book with red-brown paper covers. [20] leaves of unlined paper, 20.6 x 16.6 cm. Text in Joyce's hand in black ink on rectos, with pencil and ink revisions in margins and on facing versos. The rectos are numbered 1-20 by Joyce.
Cornell MS 4609-56A:
School exercise book with purple paper covers. [8] leaves of unlined paper, 25 x 9 cm. Text in Joyce's hand in black ink on rectos, with pencil and ink revisions in margins and on facing versos. The rectos are numbered 21-28 by Joyce.
Cornell MS 4609-56B:
School exercise book with purple paper covers. [8] leaves of unlined paper, 25 x 9 cm. Text in Joyce's hand in black ink on rectos, with pencil and ink revisions in margins and on facing versos. The rectos are numbered 29-35 by Joyce. (We follow Joyce's numbering.)

    MS:Cornell 56A, fols. 21, 21v-22, 22v-23, 24, 24v-25, 25v-26, 26v-27, 27v-28
    MS:Cornell 56B, fols. 29, 29v-30, 30v-31, 31v-32, 32v-33, 33v-34, 34v-35
  • Reproduced: JJA 13:175-243
