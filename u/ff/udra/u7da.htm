Draft Analysis Aeolus

Early Drafts

u7.0 (eo-text)
First draft: missing.
  • MS: missing
u7.1 (proto-text)
Fair copy (missing).
  • MS: missing

Fair Copy

u7.2 (Rosenbach)
Final fair copy (ink); Zurich, mid-August 1918
  • MS:Rosenbach Museum, fols. numbered 1-32
  • Reproduced: A Facsimile of the Manuscript (Faber, 1975): vol. I
  • Notebooks used: JN1 (NLI.2A), UN1 (NLI.3), UN2 (VI.D.7), UN3 (VIII.A.5)

Typescript

u7.3   (Gabler—B)
First typescript, missing; early 1918
  • MS: missing
u7.4   (Gabler—C)
Second typescript: exemplar (missing) prepared and revised for publication in The Little Review (October 1918); revisions made early 1918.
  • MS: missing
u7.5   (Gabler—D)
Second typescript: exemplar made in 1918 but prepared for the printer of Ulysses in 1921.
  • MS Buffalo V.B.5: 78-94
  • Reproduced: JJA 12: 286-302
  • Notebooks used: UN4 (NLI.5A), UN5 (NLI.5B)

Placards and Proofs

u7.6   (Gabler—1)
First placards; (PL 14-1) dated by the printer 12 August 1921
  • MS Harvard: placards 13-1, 14-1, 15-1, 16-1 (→)
  • Reproduced: JJA 18: 3-10, 35-42, 61-68, 87
  • Notebooks used: UN4 (NLI.5A), UN5 (NLI.5B), UN6 (NLI.4)
u7.7   (Gabler—2)
Second placards; (PL 13-2) dated by the printer 23 August 1921
  • MS Harvard: placards 13-2, 14-2, 15-2, 16-2 (→)
  • Reproduced: JJA 18: 13-20, 43-50, 69-76, 95
  • Notebooks used: UN4 (NLI.5A), UN6 (NLI.4)
u7.8   (Gabler—3)
Third placards; extends only as far as U84 7.1041 (remainder of text is carried over from previous draft); (PL 13-3) dated by the printer 12 September 1921
  • MS Harvard: placards 13-3, 13a-3, 14-3, 15-3
  • Reproduced: JJA 18: 21-27, 29-32, 51-58, 77-84
  • Notebooks used: UN4 (NLI.5A), UN5 (NLI.5B), UN6 (NLI.4), UN7 (V.A.2)
u7.9   (Gabler—4)
First page proofs
  • MS Buffalo V.C.1: gatherings (←) 7a, 8a, 9a (→)
  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 2, 7-22, 57-71
  • Notebooks used: UN4 (NLI.5A), UN5 (NLI.5B), UN6 (NLI.4), UN7 (V.A.2)
u7.10   (Gabler—5)
Second page proofs
  • MS Buffalo V.C.1: gatherings (←) 7b, 8b, 9b (→)
  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 3, 23-38, 73-87
  • Notebooks used: UN4 (NLI.5A), UN5 (NLI.5B)*, UN6 (NLI.4), UN7 (V.A.2)
u7.11   (Gabler—6)
Third page proofs; (PP 7) is dated by the printer 3 October 1921 and by other 6 October 1921 and marked “bon à tirer” (as is PP 9 but dated 7 October 1921)
  • MS Texas: gatherings (←) 7, 8, 9 (→)
  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 4, 39-54,89-103
u7.11+   (Gabler—6)
Late correction “Gray” to “Grey” in note dated 11 October 1921
  • MS Texas
  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 363
