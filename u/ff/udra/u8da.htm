Fair Copy

u8.2 (Rosenbach)
Final fair copy (ink); Zurich, late October 1918
  • MS:Rosenbach Museum, fols. numbered 1-29
  • Reproduced: A Facsimile of the Manuscript (Faber, 1975): vol. I
  • Notebooks used: UN1 (NLI.3), UN2 (VI.D.7), UN3 (VIII.A.5)

Typescript

u8.3   (Gabler—B)
First typescript, missing; early 1918
  • MS: missing
  • Notebooks used: UN1 (NLI.3), UN2 (VI.D.7)
u8.4   (Gabler—C)
Second typescript: exemplar (missing) prepared and revised for publication in The Little Review (February-March 1919); revisions made early 1918.
  • MS: missing
  • Notebooks used: UN2 (VI.D.7)
u8.5   (Gabler—D)
Second typescript: exemplar made in 1918 but prepared for the printer of Ulysses in 1921.
  • MS Buffalo V.B.6: 95-106, 106bis, 107-112
  • Reproduced: JJA 12: 304-322
  • Notebooks used: UN4 (NLI.5A)

  • MS Buffalo V.C.1: gatherings (←) 9b, 10b, 11b (→)
  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 88, 123-138, 177-190
  • Notebooks used: UN4 (NLI.5A), UN5 (NLI.5B)

Third page proofs; (PP 9) is dated by other 7 October 1921; (PP 10) is dated by the printer 3 October 1921, and by other 7 October and marked “bon à tirer”
  • MS Texas gatherings (←) 9, 10, MS Buffalo V.C.1: 11d (→)
  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 104, 139-140, 145-158, 193-207
u8.11   (Gabler—6)
Fourth page proofs; covers only U84 8.64-101 (=page 146) and U84 8.640-end (=PP 11); (PP 11) dated by printer 7 October 1921 and marked “bon à tirer”

  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 142, 209-223
u8.11+   (Gabler—6)

  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 141

  • MS Texas: page 146, gathering 11 (→)
  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 143, 225-239

  • MS Texas
  • Reproduced: JJA 23: 363
