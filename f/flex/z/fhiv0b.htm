For her pity there were times she even pitied the damned old devil himself fanning himself with his asbestos slippers in the coolingroom in hellº

For her charityº one day when it was sneezing cold she met a beggar girl in the park and, having no small change about her, she went behind a bramblebush, slipped off her petticoat and gave it to the beggargirl who instantly disappeared (she having been in point of fact Saint Dympna) along with the petticoat. On another occasion there was a pestilence caused by a certain dragon who said it would go on for ever unless she took off all her clothes and walked from Cape Clear to Mizzen head. So she did this and everybody pulled down all the blinds in Ireland. The dragon was then converted and entered a convent.
