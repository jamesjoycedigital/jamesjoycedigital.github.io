St kevin born on the Island of Ireland goes to lough Glendalough to live on an Island in the the lake and as there is a pond on the Island and a little island in the pond he builds
{ms, 43}
a his hut on the islet and then scoops out the floor to a dept of one foot after which he goes to the brink of the
{ms, 44}
pond and fills his tub with water which he emptys time after time into a cavity of his hut thereby forming a pool having done which he half fills the tub sets it in the
{ms, 45}
middle of the pool, pulls up his frock and seats himself in the tub and mediates meditates with burning zeal the sacrament of baptism or regeneration by water
