{ms, 24}

1 Kevin born on the island of Ireland in the Irish ocean having been granted privilege of a portable altar goes to

2 Lough Glendalough between rivers where pious Kevin lives alone on

3 an isle in the lake on which isle a plot perrymeteredº with 5 watercourses is

4 a pond in which is

5 an islet whereon holy Kevin builds a beehivehut the floor of which most holy Kevin excavates to a depth of one foot which done venerable Kevin goes to the lakeside and fills time after time a tub with water which time after time most venerable Kevin empties into

6 the cavity of his hut thereof creating a pool having done which blessed Kevin half fills the tub once with water

7 which tub then most blessed Kevin sets in the centre of the pool after which

8 Saint Kevin girds up his frock to his loins and seats himself, blessed S. Kevin, in his circumferential hiptubbath where, Doctor solitarius, he meditates with ardour

9 the sacrament of baptism or the regeneration of man by water.
