Stand forth (for no longer will I employ the inspired form of the third person singular but address myself to you direct) stand forth, come boldly, jolly me, move me to laughter in your true coloursº ere you be put back for ever till I give you your talkingto! Shem Macadamson, you know me & I know you & all yr shemeries! How have you been all this quite a while while, my tooralladdy? Let us hear how you have been enjoying yourself all the morning Since your last bad confession. You were bred, fed, fostered and fattened from holy childhood up in this 2 easter island on the piejaw of a hilarious heaven and a roaring hellº and now, forsooth, a nogger among the blankards of this dastard century, you have become a raw doubter in all known & unknown gods, nay, condemned fool, anarch, egoarch, heresiarch, you have reared your disunited kingdom on the void of your far more than dubious soul. While yet an adolescent (what do I say?), while still puerile in your tubsuit with button legs you received a gift of syringe & twin feedersº (you know, my friend, to your cost as I do the mechanism I am now poking at & don't try to hide it) that you might repopulate the land of your birth & count up your progeny by the hundred and the hundred thousand but you have thwarted the pious wish of your godmother on countless occasions of failing and have added the morosity of your delectations to Lubbock's pleasures of life inflicting your apology, even when legibly depressed upon defenceless paper & thereby adding to the unhappiness of this our popeyed world, scribblative! and this too with cantreds of overplussing sisters, the many & the chosen, congested around & about you for acres
