Good Shaun, as I was shortly before that made aware, next halted to take his breath & to loosen both bruised boots at the weir by Lazar's Walk about 9 score or so barrelhours' distance as truly he merited to do. He was there, I cd see, perspiring but happy, propped up against a slumbering warden of the peace, 1 comestabulish Sigurdsen, who had fallen asleep at the curing station in the embraces of a confiscated bottle. Now, there were as many as 29 daughters out of the national hedgeschool (for I seem to remember how I was in a look before you leap year) learning their lesson of life there, seated as they were, upon the brink, attracted to the sight of the 1st human landmark, paddling away with their 8 & 50 pedals in charming concert to the snores of the log who looked stuck to the sod as ever & anon he murmoaned,

Shaun (after he had 1st bowed to all the others in that chorus of praise of goodwill girls, asking kindly after their healths, out of pure human respect for he was the most purely human creature that was ever called man) easily recognised made out the features of his dear sister Izzy nor cd he ever after forget her as he was, besides that, her godfather as well after all.

— Sister dearest, Shaun delivered himself, as he began to take leave of her express with fondest affection, I honestly believe you will sorely miss us yet we feel, as a martyr to the discharge of duty, that it is about time we wd go. This is the result of your teachings in which we were raised, you, Sis, that used to write to us the nice letters and be telling us your oldworld tales of derringdo, you angel in our house. During

our brief absence. B be true to as many of the 10 commandments as possible. R Never miss your last mass. Never eat bad eatº of a good Friday. Never let a hog of the hill trample down your lily of the valley. Never play ladies' games on the Lord's day. Especially beware please of being a party to any demoralising home life. It saps a chap. No lowcut shirtwaists. Don't acquire a penchant for the fag habit. Have your eye out for furnished lodgers paying for meals on tally with company and piano music, the too friendly friend type. Dip into the lives of the saints in fortnightly instalments for the betterment of your mind. Keep cool as a cucumber your preserved chastity. Rather than part with that vestalite emerald of the first importance which you have where your nether extremities meet, let the entire ekumene universe perish to pot first in a pitfall first a 1000 times over. O be careful during this lenten pastoral season when the spring is in the bubbling. Times and oft shall I think of you far away on your pillow whilst amid the music of the double doorknockers. Little girl from Liffeyside you fill a big corner in my heart. O, heaven, how I shall kiss you, if I live, put my arm in yours and kiss you when cherries next come back immediately following my safe return to ignorance and bliss when we shall show our kindness by adopting all the poorest babes possible. We will render social service and help clean up things. Look at the mud of Harrington street. Bear in mind all the banana peels along Henry, Moore, Earl & Talbot streets. Stand on, say, Aston's Quay & take a good gaze into any adjacent shopwindow you choose & in about 20 minutes' time turn round & you will see you will be coated with slush occasioned by the traffic in transit. Or, better still account to me for the fact that if you purchase any fish and vegetables you fancy from the same perambulator and, having deposited the mackerel, let us say, as well as the cauliflower upon either of the edges of the two sidewalks, you take your stand in some nearby hallway to carefully note the result the restingplaces selected are visited in rotation by so many members of the canine tribe.

Sis dearest, it is my grief to go on this benedictine
{ms, 31}
errand but it is grand to be going to meet a king, not a king merely by surname, but the great king of Greater Dublin. I only wish everyone was as sure of anything in this present world as I am of everything in the one to come. Tell mother that. And now let ye create no scenes. Cheer up all round, let ye, till the grim reaper comes along as a blessing in disguise. No lonesome stuff! Drink it off, ladies, please. Parting's fun. Soon shall we be dead & happy together as you all know in the land of lost of time. One or two tears are all there is to it & then in a click of the clock off we pop en route for His Blessed Majesty, our longlived Lord.
