It was of a night at a long time agone when Adam was delvin and his madame spinning watersilts and Sir Howther had his burnt head high up in his lamphouse, laying cold hands on himself. And his two little jiminies, cousins of ours, Tristopher and Hilary, were not yet kickaheeling on the oilcloth flure of his homerigh, cashel and earthenhouse.
