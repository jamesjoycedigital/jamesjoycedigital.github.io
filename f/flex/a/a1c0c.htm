The Annals bring how

1132 AB Men like to ants wondern all over on a groot Wide Wallfisch that lay in a Runnel and. Blubby wares upat Eblanium.

566 B.A. On Ballfireeve of this year a crone that hadde a wickered kish for to hale dead turves from the bog lookit under the blay of her kish as she ran & found herself sackvulle of swart goody quickenshoon & smalle illigant brogues. Bluchy works at Hurdlesford.
