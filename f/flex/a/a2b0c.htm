It was one night at a long time ago when Adam was delvin & his madamene spinning watersilts Sir Howther had his burnt head up in his lamphouse laying cold hands on himself. And his two little jimminies were not yet kicking on the oilcloth of the van homerigh homecashel earthenhouse, Tristopher & Hilary. With their dummy. And who come to the keep of his inn but the niece of his prankwench. And the prankwench picked a rosy one & made her wit forenenst the dour. And spoke she to the dour in petty perusienne: Why do I like a poss of porterpease. But the dour handworded her grace in dootch nossow: Shut.
