sheº was the beautiful four sisters and that was her name and they used to be getting up at all hours every night when they couldn't sleep changing beds and looking everywhere for the rancers in all the fathoms and then they had their night tentacles and there they used to be all hanging around all the waists of the ships the steamships and their bottlegreen eyes and peering in through the steaming windows into the honeymoon cabins on board the big steamadories and the saloon ladies toilet chambers and rubbing off the salty cataract off the windows listening to see all the hunnishmooners and all the toilet ladies in their familiarities saying their grace before steamadory so pass the the chance for Chame sake Amen and all trembling and shaking and they used to be counting all their peributtons one four to remember her beautiful name in the dream Greg and Doug and poor Greg the four sisters and there she was now the lovely lady bawn asthore as in days of yore of planxty Gregory they used to be always singing round the wet fire with their poor feet asleep in their blankets and shawls and their bowl of stale bread and milky and waiting for poor Mucus to pass the teeth for Chokesake Amenschtrk when they all had the phlegmish whooping cough after eating a bad cramp and their farthing dip and reading a word or two out of their old book through their green spentacles and singing so now they started singing their steamadorion and old Luke for auld luke syne and she wail a cupboar coiner set on the praze savole shanghai.
