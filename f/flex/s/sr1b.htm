Leave sis to cease. Spake us sooth, folly volanty! Rustle low, now! Were you there? Where who were?

Where you there, holly and ivy, where you there when he? Gan you climb yon creeper's wall? Cling to it, mantle him, when fall the leaves then yule remember. Were you in your prickly hedges, redcoat robins, roundface chumberries? Were you hid in eyeful hollow by hollow deeping down deeping lane? Answer by your numbers, one to three.
