Let's see. See you. All seeing together. Sirs? Where saw him. So and so. Buzz, cease to cease. Begin you, Spake truth to sooth none other. Were you there? Were we where?

Were you there, holly and ivy, where you were when he? Did you climb the creeper's wall, cling to it, remember now, the then Yule remember me. Why were you in the prickly hedges, redcoat robin, roundface chumberries? Answer by your numbers. Were you there, in the eyeful hollow? We only had a hazelight hadn't we, jimmy, it was him gave us woodbines, old honeysuckler, that's what our nutty john calls him, when he puts a question touching whether the park rangers were they where trespassing on their duty. the gentleman who passed us, a Mr Beardall, a burgomaster, after which, notwithstanding it was low visibility, we had seen all we desired only our jimmy, he's a roman,

Nobody's here to hear, only trees, such as these, such as those waving there, the Barketree, O'Brientree, the Rowantree, O'Corneltree, all the trees of the wood that trembold, humbild, when they read the stoppress in the Someday's Herold.
