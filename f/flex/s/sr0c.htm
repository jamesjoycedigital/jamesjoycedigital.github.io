Let's see. See yews. All see, saw together. Hers? Him saw hymn. So and so. Hers, cease to cease. Kindly turn the new leaves, thinning over, lisperingly. Cease to cease. Rustle off now. Spake the sooth folly volanty to us. Were you there? Where who were?

Were you there, holly and ivy, where you were when he? Gan you climb yon creeper's wall? Cling to it, mantle him, then Yule remember me. Were you in your prickly hedges, redcoat robins, roundface chumberries? Answer by your numbers 1 to 3. Were you there, in the eyeful hollow in hollow deeping in deeping lane? Haze! We had only half a hazelight to see, me & my auxy, Jim Darcy, hadn't we, jimmy, it was him, no kidding, stood us mountjoys nutty woodbines, free, rare Sish! old honeysuckler, that's what my young lady here, that's Fred Watkins all the way from blank, jock, our john, he was, calls him, when we sort of gathered he was in sugar, he puts us a certain question touching whether the phoenix rangers were they forgiving his trespassing. Pees! the gentleman who passed us, a Mr Beardall, a burgomaster, after which, notwithstanding it was low visibility, we had seen all we desired only our jimmy, he's a roman,
{ms, 9}
says, Spiss! let's spy on that old honeysuckler after His Corkiness Exexex paid for the 3 bottles of joy, and his fino oloroso. Following idly, what did you see? Holymerry, iveysad, were you there? Nobody's here to hear, only trees, such as these, such as those waving there, the Barketree, the O'Brientree, the Rowantree, the O'Corneltree, all the trees of the wood that trembold, humbild, when they heard the stoppress from their Someday's Herold.
