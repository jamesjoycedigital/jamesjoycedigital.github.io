High hellskirt saw ladies hen smoke a lily young pigger.

Jeg elske således en smukke lille Pige.

…. legging a jig or two to show them how to shake their benders & the dainty how to bring to mind the gladdest garments out of sight & all the way of a maid with a man & making a sort of a cackling noise like two and a penny or half a crown & holding up a silver shiner. Well, of all the ones ever I heard! To any lass at all of no matter what sex a a half a crown a go to sit and make fun in Humpy's lap!
