— But have I asked you, dear Shaun, I remembered, who it was who gave you the permit?

— Then, I explained, you might be so by order.

— Forgive me, Shaun replied, it was made known in the prophecies and there is a power over me that is put upon me from on high & I am hopeless to do anything about it.

— Dear Shaun, I agreed, what then were it you who will maybe bear those letter?

— As, Shaun replied, to that I have the power.
