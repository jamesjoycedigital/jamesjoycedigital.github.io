As a matter of fact I undertake to discontinue all practices and and honestly as my policy I have had my lesson. I will say that since my toils of domestication began famine has receded from this land. Idle were it to inquire whether I be the product of team work or an outcome of group marriage for, surrounded by obscurity, I claim my natural right at common law to opt for blank. I mean to say, had my faithful Fulvia turned her back on her ways in search for brunette men or had she left her crocus bed at the mere suggestion of some infamous fisherman there might accrue advantage to ask but she ever did ensue whatsoever pertained unto fairhood & I did encompass her about, my verminbreeder, with lovingkindness and with soft goods & hardware and fine hosiery lines & geegaws such as women wear
