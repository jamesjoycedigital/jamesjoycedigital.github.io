There is not a teaspoonspill of evidence to my bad and I can so protest against publication of libel by any Ticks Tipsylon to the highest personage at this moment holding down the throne. As a matter of fact I undertake to discontinue all practices and and honestly as my best policy I have had my lesson. The amusing part is I will say that since my toils of domestication began famine has receded from this land. Idle were it to inquire whether I be the mass product of team work or an outcome of group marriage for, surrounded by obscurity, I claim my natural freeman's right at common law to opt for blank. I mean to say, had my faithful Fulvia turned her back on her ways to go on the hills in search for brunette men or had she left her crocus bed at the mere suggestion of some infamous fisherman who wd have abused of her there might accrue advantage to ask but she ever did ensue whatsoever pertained unto fairhood & I did take the hand of my delights, my jealousy & did lead her to Ringsend ferry whimpering I did encompass her all about, my verminbreeder, with lovingkindness and I gave unto my little turkeythighs with soft goods & hardware and fine hosiery lines & geegaws such as women wear &
